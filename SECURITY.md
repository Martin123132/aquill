# Security And Privacy

Aquill is designed as a local-first transcription tool.

## Privacy Posture

- Do not upload private media, transcripts, archives, databases, screenshots, credentials, or generated outputs to cloud services.
- Keep local media and generated artifacts under your D-drive checkout.
- `inputs/`, `outputs/`, `data/`, `models/`, `cache/`, `tmp/`, and `web/dist/` are ignored by Git by default.
- Public examples, screenshots, or bug reports should use sanitized media and transcripts only.
